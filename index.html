About the Book

Status: Ongoing · Year: 2025 · Genre: BL, dark fantasy, romance

A reincarnated soul without memory, a god bound by sin, and a forest that remembers every vow. Snow falls in summer when the heavens lie, and petals drift when blades are drawn.

  • Worlds: Heaven, Mortal, Demon
  • Motifs: vows, memory, resurrection, lotus
  • Tone: semi-old cultivation, layered scenes

The years had not been kind to Xin Zhi.

In the Heavenly Court, whispers followed him wherever he went.

“He’s a weak god…” “Only knows how to heal scratches…” “A candle in the wind—better snuffed out than sheltered.”

Xin Zhi smiled softly, as he always did, and let the words pass through him like rain on stone… (sample trimmed for page length)
